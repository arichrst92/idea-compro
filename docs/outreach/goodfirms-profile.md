GoodFirms sends a verification email + may require a phone callback. Allow 5-7 business days for profile to go live.

## Reviews

GoodFirms also allows client reviews. Same playbook as Clutch:

1. After publishing profile, invite 5 happy clients via GoodFirms dashboard.
2. Aim for 3 verified reviews in 60 days.
3. Follow up gently with each invited client.
