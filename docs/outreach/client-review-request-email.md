# Client Review Request — Email Templates

**Why it matters:** Google reviews are a direct ranking factor for the local pack. Clutch reviews are a direct ranking factor on Clutch listings and a strong trust signal for AI search engines.

**Use case:** Send to existing clients you have delivered for, where the project NPS was 9+ and the relationship is current/recent.

**Timing:** Send within 30-90 days of project completion (recency matters; older projects = lower response rate).

## Template 4 — LinkedIn recommendation request

If it'd help, here are 2-3 prompts you could use as a starting point:

- "What was the biggest concern going in, and how did IDE Asia handle it?"

## How to use these templates

### Cadence
- **Week 1 after project close**: send Template 1 (Google review).
- **Week 4 after project close**: if review not yet submitted, send a single short follow-up: "Just bumping this up — totally understand if too busy, but the link is here in case it's helpful: [link]."
- **Month 3**: if still no Google review, send Template 4 (LinkedIn recommendation) — much lower bar and many clients prefer LinkedIn anyway.
- **Month 6**: send Template 3 (Clutch) only to your top 5-10 clients — Clutch is more effort for them.

### Personalisation
The single specific compliment in the second paragraph is non-optional. Generic "thank you for working with us" emails get ~10% response. Specific, genuine compliments get 40-60%.

### What NOT to do
- Don't offer to pay for reviews (against Google + Clutch + LinkedIn policy).
- Don't pre-write the review and ask them to paste it (looks fake, often detected, embarrassing).
- Don't send to ten clients on the same day from the same email — Google flags burst-rate reviews as suspicious.
