## Reviews — the critical next step

Clutch reviews are verified by Clutch staff calling/emailing your client directly. Process:

1. Submit profile.
2. Within Clutch dashboard, add 5-10 client contacts (name, title, email, project description). Clutch reaches out to them.
3. Each verified review takes ~30-45 minutes of the client's time.
4. Aim for 5 published reviews in 90 days — this is when the profile becomes "Verified" + ranks well.

**Tactical tip:** Offer your clients a small thank-you (premium chocolates, branded gift) for completing the review — this is not against Clutch policy. Many firms see ~70% completion when there's a thoughtful follow-up.

## Sponsorship (optional)

Clutch offers paid sponsorship to rank higher on specific category pages (e.g. "Top IT Consulting Firms in Indonesia"). Typical pricing: $1,000-3,000 per category per year. Worth considering after 5+ reviews are in place.
