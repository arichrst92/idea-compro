# IDE Asia — Off-Page Outreach Package

Ready-to-use copy for the high-leverage off-page SEO actions outlined in
`docs/SEO-PLAYBOOK.md`. Each file is structured so you can copy/paste
straight into the target platform or email it as-is, with minimal edits.

Estimated effort to execute everything in this folder: 6-10 person-hours
total, spread over 2-3 weeks. Estimated month-12 impact (combined with
content + landing pages): 15-30 new referring domains, materially
improved domain rating, and trust signals visible to Google + AI search.

## Execution sequence (recommended)

### Week 1 — High-trust foundation
1. Submit to **IBM Partner Plus directory** (`ibm-partner-plus-submission.md`). This single link is the highest-trust backlink available to IDE Asia.
2. Claim **Google Business Profile** for Jakarta office (`google-business-profile-setup.md`). Critical for "IT consulting Jakarta" type queries.
3. Send **press release** to top 3 Indonesian tech publications (`press-release-EN.md` and `press-release-ID.md`).

### Week 2 — B2B directories
4. Create **Clutch.co profile** (`clutch-profile.md`). Clutch is the highest-ROI B2B directory in IT services.
5. Create **GoodFirms profile** (`goodfirms-profile.md`).
6. Send first batch of **client review request emails** (`client-review-request-email.md`). Goal: 10 reviews in 4 weeks.

### Week 3 — Content seeding
7. Publish first **LinkedIn post** from CEO (`linkedin-thought-leadership.md`).
8. Submit **Wikidata entry** for PT Solusi Inovasi Bangsa (`wikidata-entry.md`).
9. Send first **tech press pitch** to DailySocial (`tech-press-pitch.md`).

### Ongoing — sustain
- 1 LinkedIn post per week from a senior team member.
- 1 client review request per month, targeting 4-5 reviews/quarter.
- 1 contributed article per quarter in Indonesian tech press.
