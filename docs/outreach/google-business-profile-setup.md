## Verification

Google will verify ownership by:
1. **Postcard** mailed to the address (most common — 5-14 days).
2. **Phone call** with verification code (sometimes available).
3. **Email** (rare).

Until verified, profile is not publicly listed. Plan accordingly.

## Reviews strategy (critical)

GBP reviews are a major ranking factor for local pack. Target: 10 reviews in 4 weeks, 25 by month 6, 50 by month 12.

Process:
1. Identify happy clients (project NPS ≥9).
2. Send the request email from `client-review-request-email.md`.
3. Include the direct review link (Google Maps → IDE Asia → "Write a review" → copy URL).
4. Follow up once after 1 week if no review submitted.
5. Reply to every single review within 48 hours (Google tracks response rate).

**Never:**
- Buy reviews. Google detects and suppresses them.
- Offer incentives in exchange for reviews (against Google policy).
- Reply defensively to negative reviews — acknowledge, take offline, resolve.
