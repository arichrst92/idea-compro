**IDE Asia (PT Solusi Inovasi Bangsa) Achieves IBM Certified Partner Status, Expands Enterprise AI and Cloud Practice Across Southeast Asia**

*Jakarta-based IT services firm adds 30+ IBM products to its delivery portfolio, serves banking, telecommunications, and government clients across Indonesia and the region.*

**JAKARTA, [DATE] —** IDE Asia, the trading name of PT Solusi Inovasi Bangsa, has been certified as an official IBM Partner, authorizing the firm to source, deploy, integrate, and support the full IBM software and infrastructure portfolio for enterprise clients across Southeast Asia. The certification expands IDE Asia's capability to deliver IBM watsonx artificial intelligence, IBM Cloud, IBM Db2, QRadar SIEM, IBM Z mainframes, and more than thirty additional IBM products to Indonesian banks, telecommunications operators, government agencies, and large enterprises.

IDE Asia is headquartered at Graha Binakarsa, South Jakarta, with offices in Bandung, Hanoi (Vietnam), and Sydney (Australia). The firm is ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 27001:2013 certified, and was founded in 2013 with formal incorporation in 2019. Notable enterprise clients include Bank Jakarta, Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI), UOB Indonesia, Accenture, Telkomsel, Pertamina, and Semen Indonesia Group.

"As Indonesian enterprises accelerate AI adoption and cloud migration, they need a partner who understands both the technology and the local regulatory context," said [CEO NAME], CEO of IDE Asia. "Our IBM Certified Partner status, combined with our ISO 27001 controls and deep Indonesian banking and government experience, lets us deliver enterprise-grade implementations of watsonx, IBM Cloud, and the full IBM portfolio with regulatory fit-for-purpose from day one."

The firm's services span six lines: Information Technology Consulting, Information Technology Outsourcing, Information Technology Hiring, Cloud Infrastructure, Information Technology Security, and Squad-Based Delivery. Recent engagements include OJK-compliant cloud migration for an Indonesian bank, IBM watsonx.ai implementation for customer service automation, and twenty-four-hour Security Operations Center delivery using IBM QRadar SIEM.

IDE Asia is also a Deloitte Independent Contractor, engaged as a delivery partner on select Deloitte client projects across the region.
